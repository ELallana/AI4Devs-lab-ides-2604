A `candidate` has the next fields

|name|type|required|default value|restrictions|description|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|name|text|yes|||the name of the candidate|
|surname|text|yes|||the surnames of the candidate|
|email|text|yes||must comply the email address format|the email of the candidate|
|phone|text|yes||must comply the phone number format|the phone number of the candidate|
|education|text|no|||the education titles of the candidate|
|job_exp|text|no|||the job experience of the candidate|
|cv|file|no||a file of type PDF o DOCX| a file with the CV of the candidate|
